FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions advisors ask before getting started.

Who is Iceberg built for?

Boutique capital raising advisers, independent M&A advisers, placement agents running multiple live mandates, advisers who treat their network as a long-term asset, and operators who value confidence, discretion, and outcomes.

What do I need to get started?

All you need is a signed mandate and investor pitch materials from a founder ready to go live.

How long does it take to get set up?

We can have a workspace for you and your mandate live for execution within 5 minutes.

How does Iceberg fit with my current tech stack?

Iceberg sits alongside your existing tools as a deal execution layer. Your CRM, email, and finance tools stay as systems of record. We integrate with Gmail or Outlook, pull in existing contacts for continuous reprioritisation, and run deal-specific workflows without requiring you to rebuild your stack.

Do you charge a percentage of capital raised?

No. Pricing is subscription-based on capital raising throughput rather than performance fees. You keep 100% of your advisory economics.

What is included in the pilot?

The 30-day pilot includes one live mandate with full platform access, investor targeting recommendations, contact intelligence, outreach tracking, and no platform fees. You'll receive 120 free contacts and connect your Gmail and calendar.

Do you offer an enterprise plan for larger firms?

Yes. If your firm has a large contact database, multiple seats, or concurrent mandates, our Enterprise plan lets you cancel your PitchBook, Cap IQ, BuySideBack, and enrichment tool subscriptions and replace them all with Iceberg. Bulk contact packages, multi-seat access with shared network graphs, and dedicated onboarding. See Enterprise pricing →

How does Iceberg compare to PitchBook, Cap IQ, or BuySideBack?

Those tools are built for large investment banks and cost $20,000 to $40,000+/year per seat. Iceberg gives boutique advisors the same depth of contact intelligence (investor/buyer identification, enrichment, relationship mapping, and execution tracking) at a price that scales with the deals you're actually running. No upfront commitment, no enterprise contracts.

Where does Iceberg's intelligence come from?

Our reasoning layer processes engagement behaviour, relationship strength, mandate fit, check sizes, and investment focus data. Recommendations come from the global deal universe, continuously reprioritised, not static lists.

What happens with my contacts and data?

Your imported contacts remain private and fully yours. Iceberg-originated contacts strengthen the shared network. Co-developed relationships stay private while Iceberg learns anonymously. If you leave, your imported contacts are frozen and never shared.
